PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for Bennie's Bakeshop. This privacy policy applies to all personal information gathered from our website. The privacy policy is intended to inform you of the following:

1. The purpose of the collection of personal information
2. The source of the collection of personal information
3. The collection of personal information
4. The manner of the collection of personal information
5. The storage and security of personal information

Purpose of Information Collection

Bennie's Bakeshop holds personal information, and only have access to information that you voluntarily provide us through submission on our website. Bennie's Bakeshop shall ensure –

(a) that we only collect the personal information that you voluntarily agree to provide us.
(b) that the personal information that you provide us is only used to provide you access to features on our website, and provide access to any other features you sign up for or consent to use.
(c) that the personal information that you provide us is necessary for your use on our website, and enabling us to provide you with features of the website, and additional features we offer you.

Source of Information Collection

Bennie's Bakeshop only collects information that you voluntarily provide us through submission on our website. Bennie's Bakeshop does not collect any other information on you, and does not use your information other than what was agreed upon through submission of your information on our website. Bennie's Bakeshop shall ensure –

(a) that we only collect personal information that you voluntarily agree to provide us, and that this information is only collected from you within our website by submitting your personal information through submission of the online form.
(b) that all personal information collected is only from within our website, and not from any other outside sources, including third parties.
(c) that all personal information collected is only used in the instance that was agreed upon through submission of your information on our website.

Collection of Information

Bennie's Bakeshop ensures that only authorised administrative personnel are provided access to your personal information, and no others, including Bennie's Bakeshop employees, other users, and other third parties, cannot view, access or change your personal information. Bennie's Bakeshop shall ensure –

(a) that only Bennie's Bakeshop authorised administrative personnel are provided access to view, update and delete your personal information.
(b) that your personal information is not viewable and accessible to any other Bennie's Bakeshop employees, users, or viewers of the Bennie's Bakeshop website, and your personal information is not shared by, and cannot be accessed by, any third parties.
(c) that you may opt out of future contact from us at any time.
(d) that you may request, by contacting us through the email provided at the bottom of this page, the following:

(i) see what data we currently have on you
(ii) change/correct data we currently have on you
(iii) delete data we currently have on you

Manner of Information Collection

Bennie's Bakeshop is hosted in New Zealand, and therefore must abide by New Zealand law, particularly the Privacy Act 1993. Bennie's Bakeshop shall ensure –

(a) that the collection of your personal information abides by current New Zealand privacy law, under the Privacy Act 1993 and any other law particular to the collection and holding of your personal information.
(b) that all personal information collected is not accessed or provided to any third parties without your consent.
(c) that we do not use your personal information to gain from your personal circumstances, whether by age, gender, religion or location or any other circumstances.

Security of Information

Bennie's Bakeshop takes all reasonable steps and precautions to protect your information. When you submit personal information via our website, your information is protected the moment it is submitted, both online and offline. Bennie's Bakeshop shall ensue –

(a) that the information is protected, by such security safeguards as it is reasonable in the circumstances to take, against—

(i) loss
(ii) access, use, modification, or disclosure, except with authorised administrative personnel within Bennie's Bakeshop
(iii) other misuse

(b) that if it is necessary for the information to be provided to an outside source for reasons beyond our control, such as police or governmental purposes, all reasonable steps will be taken to prevent unauthorised use or unauthorised disclosure of your personal information.


If you feel we are not abiding by our privacy policy, you wish to request information regarding your personal information, or for any other privacy and security related questions, contact privacy@benniesbakeshop.co.nz
